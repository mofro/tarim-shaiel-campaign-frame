### Detailed Charm Description
Ox-Body Technique allows a character to withstand more punishment than would normally be possible. It represents the pinnacle of physical resilience, transforming the character's body into something nearly superhuman. The charm doesn't just increase health—it fundamentally alters the character's capacity to endure, reflecting years of training, natural toughness, or spiritual fortitude.
