# Graceful Crane Stance

## METADATA
**Archetype:** Warrior (primary)  
**Tier Required:** 1  
**Exalted Source:** Graceful Crane Stance (Athletics 1, Essence 1, Cost: 3m)

---

## COST

**Spend 1 Hope**  
**Type:** Reflexive

---

## PREREQUISITES

**Tier:** 1 (Daggerheart Level 1)  
**Archetype:** Warrior (primary) / Seeker (with GM approval)  
**Fictional Requirement:** Character must have demonstrated exceptional balance and body awareness (climbing, acrobatics, martial training, or similar competency)

---

## RECALL COST

**0 Hope** (Tier 1 Charms have no recall cost)

---

## EFFECT

The hero can balance on any surface that could support their weight, no matter how precarious or narrow. This includes:
- Single taut ropes or chains
- Ice-slicked surfaces
- Shifting sand or gravel
- Tree branches during windstorms
- The rim of a moving wagon wheel
- Crumbling ledges

While this Charm is active, the hero cannot be knocked down or lose their footing through mundane means. Environmental penalties to movement are ignored. The effect lasts for one scene or until the hero chooses to end it.

**Duration:** One scene (or until dismissed)

---

## TIER SCALING

**Tier 1 (Level 1):**  
Base effect as written above. Hero maintains perfect balance on any weight-bearing surface.

**Tier 2 (Levels 2-4):**  
**Enhanced:** The hero can now balance on surfaces that *barely* support weight (single strands of silk, water surface for one step, falling debris). Additionally, while active, the hero gains +1 to Evasion.

**Tier 3 (Levels 5-7):**  
**Improved:** Evasion bonus increases to +2. The hero can activate this Charm as an interrupt even when falling.

**Tier 4 (Levels 8-10):**  
**Mastery:** The hero can balance on things that provide no physical support at all (smoke, moonlight, sound waves) for brief moments (1-2 heartbeats per step). Evasion bonus increases to +3.

---

## CHARM TELL

**Everyone sees:** The hero's movements become impossibly fluid. Their feet seem to barely touch surfaces, and their body naturally finds perfect equilibrium even in chaotic motion. A faint shimmer—like heat haze—follows their footsteps.

**Only wielder hears:** The tool whispers in the warrior's original combat language (the tongue they learned to fight in): *"Trust your body. It remembers what your mind doubts."*

---

## WHISPER INTERACTION

### When Following Guidance:
**Dice:** Use celestial advantage when applicable to movement rolls (2d12 Hope + 1d12 Fear, keep highest Hope die at Tier 1)

**Additional Effect:** The hero moves with complete confidence. Any allies watching gain +1 Hope from witnessing such assured movement.

**Narrative Feel:** The warrior's body moves before thought—pure trained reflex. Doubt vanishes. There is only motion, breath, and the surface beneath their feet.

### When Resisting Guidance:
**Dice:** Standard duality dice (1d12 Hope + 1d12 Fear) when applicable to movement rolls

**Bonus:** Gain +1 Hope (boundary-setting reward)

**Effect:** Charm still works normally (perfect balance maintained), but without celestial dice advantage. The hero consciously thinks through each movement rather than trusting muscle memory.

**Narrative Feel:** The warrior second-guesses their footing, mentally calculating each step. The balance is maintained through conscious effort rather than instinctive grace.

---

## GUIDANCE DEPENDENCY

- [x] **Standard Charm:** Works regardless of following/resisting (whisper adds optional bonus)
- [ ] **Guidance-Dependent:** Requires following whisper to activate at all
- [ ] **Knowledge Charm:** Provides information + whispered interpretation

## TACTICAL EXAMPLES

### Combat Scenario:
```
Context: Rooftop chase across rain-slicked tiles, pursuing assassin
Player: "I activate Graceful Crane Stance as I leap onto the steep roof"
GM: "Your weapon whispers in your mind: 'Trust your body. It remembers.'"
Player: "I follow the guidance—I'm not thinking, just moving"
Dice: 2d12 Hope + 1d12 Fear (keep highest Hope die + Fear die)
Outcome: Player rolls 11 Hope, 4 Fear—success with Hope. Not only do they land perfectly on the slick tiles, but nearby guard NPC gains +1 Hope from witnessing such impossible grace
Hope Tracking: -1 Hope spent (activation), +1 Hope gained (from roll), net 0
```

### Non-Combat Scenario:
```
Context: Crossing a rope bridge during earthquake, need to reach injured companion on far side
Player: "I use Graceful Crane Stance to cross despite the shaking"
GM: "The bridge lurches violently. Your tool whispers: 'Trust your body.'"
Player: "Actually, no—I resist. I'm going to consciously plan each step because my friend's life depends on this"
GM: "You gain +1 Hope for setting that boundary. Make your Athletics roll"
Dice: 1d12 Hope + 1d12 Fear (standard, no advantage)
Outcome: Player crosses safely but more slowly, conscious thought guiding each placement
Hope Tracking: -1 Hope spent (activation), +1 Hope gained (resisting whisper), net 0
```

## DESIGN NOTES

**Thematic Fit:** Embodies Warrior archetype's core tension—trusting physical training when psychological doubt creeps in. The perfect balance represents the hero's *earned* capabilities that doubt cannot erase.

**Balance:** 1 Hope cost makes this frequently usable (2-3 times per session with Hope generation). Reflexive activation allows defensive use. Tier scaling provides clear power growth without breaking early-game balance.

**R/H/K Connection:**  
- **Resist:** Tool warns against overthinking in combat—trust the trained body
- **Hunger:** Tool calls for use when hero doubts their physical prowess
- **Know:** Fragmentary memory—*"You stood on the mountain's edge and never feared falling. Why fear now?"*
