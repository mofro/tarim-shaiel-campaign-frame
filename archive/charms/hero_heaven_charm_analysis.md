# HERO HEAVEN CHARM SYSTEM: Exalted Mapping & Adaptation Analysis

**Goal:** Compare Exalted's Charm structure to Tarim-Shaiel's needs. Decide: adopt wholesale, simplify, or create hybrid?

---

## PART 1: ARCHETYPE ↔ EXALTED TYPE MAPPING

### WARRIOR ↔ SOLAR EXALTED

**Similarity:** Martial excellence. Pure combat focus. No supernatural subterfuge.

**Key Trait:** Everything a Warrior does is *visible and direct*. Their power is undeniable.

**Sample Charm Progression:**

**Session 2-3 (First Charm Recovery):**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** MANY-ATTACKS TECHNIQUE (Close Combat 3)
  - Cost: 2 motes (immediately spent)
  - Effect: Make second attack in same flurry
  - Variants: No dice penalty (Solar), reduced flurry penalty (Infernal), substitute action (Lunar)

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** WARRIOR'S DOUBLE STRIKE
  - Cost: 1 Hope (mechanical clarity: visible and immediate)
  - Effect: Make a second attack against same or different target
  - Warrior variant: No penalty on second attack
  - Bridge variant: Only second attack if first hit heals/protects ally instead of damages
  - Activation: "I hear the blade's hunger for another strike"

**Session 5-6 (Mid-Power Charms):**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** BULWARK STANCE (Close Combat 2 base, but scaling effect)
  - Cost: 1 mote per use
  - Effect: Reset Defense OR clear penalties
  - Narrative: Warrior flows into defensive posture

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** WARRIOR'S READINESS
  - Cost: 1 Hope to activate OR (optional) follow blade's "protect" whisper
  - Effect: Reset Defense penalties, or increase Defense by +1 for rest of combat
  - Narrative: Hero discovers: "I'm not just about attacking. I can defend without losing power"
  - R/H/K Discovery: Warrior might initially think this is defensive cowardice, discovers it's wisdom

- **Exalted Equivalent:** HEAVENLY GUARDIAN DEFENSE (Close Combat 5, Essence 2)
  - Cost: 2 motes per use
  - Effect: Apply Defense against environmental hazards (falling, traps, disease)
  - Once per session with Stunt: escape collapsing building, parry ground during fall

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** WARRIOR'S TRANSCENDENT DEFENSE
  - Cost: 2 Hope (significant investment)
  - Effect: Apply Defense against non-combat hazards (environmental, traps, poison)
  - Narrative: "My blade protects not just from enemies, but from the world's cruelty"
  - R/H/K Discovery: Warrior realizes power extends beyond combat

**Session 8 (High-Power Charms):**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** IRON WHIRLWIND ATTACK (Close Combat 5 prerequisite)
  - Cost: Spend 3 motes
  - Effect: Make flurry of attacks without standard penalties
  - Narrative: Warrior becomes whirlwind of blades

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** WARRIOR'S CELESTIAL FURY
  - Cost: 3 Hope (or follow the blade's hunger for total destruction; risky)
  - Effect: Make up to Essence/2 additional attacks in single round (consuming full action)
  - Each attack after first: -1 to-hit penalty (instead of normal flurry penalties)
  - Narrative: "I am a god of war"
  - R/H/K Discovery: Warrior faces question: "Am I using this power to protect, or have I become the threat?"

### BREAKER ↔ SOLAR/INFERNAL HYBRID

**Similarity:** Destruction and system-breaking. Visible, forceful, transformative. But *morally ambiguous*.

**Key Trait:** Breaker's power is about *undoing*, not building. This creates self-doubt.

**Sample Charm Progression:**

**Session 2-3:**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** TERRAIN-ALTERING ADVANTAGE (Craft 5, but scaled down)
  - Cost: 1 mote
  - Effect: Create environmental hazard/difficult terrain; 2 bonus successes on Prepare roll

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** BREAKER'S LIBERATION WAVE
  - Cost: 1 Hope
  - Effect: Destroy a structure, wall, or obstacle in immediate area; allies gain +2 to escape/move
  - Whisper: "Tear it down. It's oppressive."
  - Narrative: Feels clearly aligned. "I'm breaking chains!"
  - R/H/K Discovery (Session 4-5): Later uses make Breaker question—was that building actually oppression, or just an obstacle?

**Session 5-6:**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** BUREAU-BREAKING METHOD (Embassy 3)
  - Cost: 1 mote
  - Effect: Sabotage bureaucratic system; introduce obstacles equal to Essence

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** BREAKER'S SYSTEMATIC UNRAVELING
  - Cost: 1 Hope (or follow corruption-hunger whisper for +1 effectiveness)
  - Effect: Disable or break one system/organization activity; add difficulties to their future actions
  - Whisper: "Unravel their power. Expose their corruption."
  - Narrative R/H/K Discovery Moment:
    - Session 2-3: "Systems ARE oppressive. This is clean."
    - Session 4-5: "Wait... what if I break something that was actually holding people together?"
    - Session 6-7: "I'm starting to understand: destruction is easy. Building from wreckage is hard."

**Session 8:**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** ELEMENTAL FURY (Dragon-Blooded variant, but for destructive power)
  - Cost: 3 Hope + powerful commitment
  - Effect: Massive destructive wave; lasting environmental damage
  - Essence scaling: Duration increases, damage increases

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** BREAKER'S APOCALYPTIC POTENTIAL
  - Cost: 3 Hope (or follow blade's HUNGER for apocalypse—risky, loses Hope return)
  - Effect: Destroy everything in area (buildings, obstacles, structures); create lasting hazard zone
  - Narrative: "I can level cities. I remember being able to reshape the world."
  - R/H/K Crisis: Breaker faces existential question:
    - "Am I a liberator or a destroyer?"
    - "Did I destroy the Empire, or did I destroy the people I was supposed to save?"
    - "If I have THIS power, how do I use it without becoming a tyrant?"

### BRIDGE ↔ SIDEREAL EXALTED

**Similarity:** Connection, fate-weaving, seeing strings between people. Subtle. Power through understanding relationships.

**Key Trait:** Bridge's power is about *connecting and mediating*. But also about *manipulation*.

**Sample Charm Progression:**

**Session 2-3:**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** WITNESS RAPPORT TECHNIQUE (Awareness 1)
  - Cost: Commit 1 mote while questioning
  - Effect: Trivial characters answer all questions truthfully

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** BRIDGE'S TRUTH-READING
  - Cost: 1 Hope (or follow blade's "understand them" whisper)
  - Effect: When in conversation, ask 3 questions; NPC must answer truthfully
  - Whisper: "See into their heart. Know what they fear."
  - Narrative: Bridge thinks this is empathy. "I'm understanding people better!"

**Session 5-6:**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** MOTIVE-DISCERNING TECHNIQUE (Embassy 2 or Integrity 2)
  - Cost: Commit 1 mote per question
  - Effect: Ask questions about target's motivations; Storyteller answers truthfully

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** BRIDGE'S ULTIMATE UNDERSTANDING
  - Cost: 1 Hope per question (can ask up to Essence questions)
  - Effect: Understand target's primary motivation, deepest fear, greatest desire
  - Whisper (getting darker): "Know them. Control them. Make them need you."
  - R/H/K Discovery Moment (Session 5-6):
    - Session 2: "I'm just understanding people."
    - Session 5: "Wait... I'm using their fears against them. Is that still bridging?"
    - Session 6: "Have I become a manipulator? Or is understanding how to influence them the same as healing?"

**Session 8:**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** PARALYZED COMMITTEE INFLICTION (Sidereal)
  - Cost: Commit 1 mote for rest of story
  - Effect: Declare target without acting; all their ventures suffer consequences

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** BRIDGE'S PUPPET-MASTER REVELATION
  - Cost: 2 Hope (or follow blade's hunger to "own them")
  - Effect: For rest of scene, target's motivations are transparent; Bridge can influence decisions subtly
  - Essence scaling: Duration extends; can affect multiple targets
  - Whisper (whispered in the dark): "You know them completely. You ARE them now."
  - R/H/K Crisis: Bridge faces horrifying realization:
    - "I don't bridge people. I pull their strings."
    - "Do I actually believe in connection, or do I just want power over people?"
    - "If I can see and manipulate everyone... am I still human? Or am I a puppet master hiding behind words of healing?"

### SEEKER ↔ SIDEREAL/LUNAR HYBRID

**Similarity:** Knowledge, discovery, adaptation. Finding patterns. Seeing what's hidden.

**Key Trait:** Seeker's power reveals *truth*. But what is truth? Objective fact or tool?

**Sample Charm Progression:**

**Session 2-3:**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** AS THE CROW FLIES (Navigate 1)
  - Cost: Always active
  - Effect: Always know shortest route

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** SEEKER'S REVELATION PATH
  - Cost: None (passive/always active)
  - Effect: Always know the shortest path to any known location; can cut corners = Essence times without penalty
  - Narrative: "Knowledge shows me the way"

**Session 5-6:**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** JADE LEAVES A TRAIL (Awareness 2 or Embassy 2)
  - Cost: Commit 1 mote for scene
  - Effect: Ask "Who does target work for?" and similar power-structure questions

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** SEEKER'S POWER-TRACING
  - Cost: 1 Hope
  - Effect: Track influence and power structures; identify who answers to whom; see political/economic connections
  - Whisper: "See the web. Know who controls whom."
  - Narrative: Seeker feels like solving a puzzle. "Knowledge is power!"
  - R/H/K Discovery (Session 5-6):
    - Session 2: "I'm just finding information."
    - Session 5: "Information I find... people act on it. I'm not neutral. I'm a weapon."

**Session 8:**
- **Exalted Equivalent:** SAGACIOUS READING OF INTENT (Awareness 3 or Integrity 3)
  - Cost: Commit 1 mote for scene; spend motes for questions
  - Effect: Read text deeply; understand author's emotions, intimacies, beliefs

- **Hero Heaven Translation:** SEEKER'S ABSOLUTE KNOWLEDGE
  - Cost: 2 Hope per revelation
  - Effect: Read any text, scroll, or artifact; understand its purpose, creator's intent, all secrets within
  - Essence scaling: Can read minds directly (with Charm upgrade at Session 8+)
  - Whisper (seductive): "All secrets are mine now. No one can hide from you."
  - R/H/K Crisis: Seeker faces knowledge paradox:
    - "I know everything about everyone. Is that enlightenment or tyranny?"
    - "Did I seek knowledge to help, or to have power over people?"
    - "If I know all secrets... do I still deserve the trust people give me?"

## PART 2: COST STRUCTURE COMPARISON

### EXALTED'S SYSTEM

**Cost Types:**
1. **Spend X motes** — immediate, one-time cost (motes don't regenerate instantly in combat)
2. **Commit X motes** — lock in for duration (scene, story, until ended)
3. **Reflex/Simple/Dramatic actions** — action economy cost
4. **Essence prerequisites** — scaling gates (only Essence 2+ Exalts access high-power Charms)

**Example:**
```
MANY-ATTACKS TECHNIQUE (Close Combat 3)
- Spend 2 motes on Step 1
- Make second attack without flurry penalty
- Cost is IMMEDIATE and VISIBLE (table sees 2 motes spent)
```

**Scaling Pattern:**
- Low tier: 1-2 mote Spend (immediate, small cost)
- Mid tier: 1-2 mote Commit (longer duration) OR 2-3 mote Spend
- High tier: 2-3 mote Spend OR Commit 1 mote for whole story

**Regeneration:** Motes regenerate at 1-3 per turn based on Essence. (Essence 1 = 1/turn, Essence 2 = 2/turn, etc.)

---

### HERO HEAVEN'S SYSTEM (What We Know)

**What we've established:**
- Hope is the visible resource (gained/lost mechanically)
- Charms cost Hope (visible to table)
- R/H/K framework affects Hope access (following guidance = loss OR gain depending on alignment)

**What we haven't specified:**
- Do Charms have ongoing costs (like Commit)?
- Does essence regenerate or reset per scene?
- How does activation work mechanically?

## PART 3: PRACTICAL COMBAT FLOW COMPARISON

### SCENARIO: Session 2 Combat (Low Power)

**Hero:** Warrior with Sword
**Opponent:** Brigand (roughly equal)
**Hero's Charm:** WARRIOR'S DOUBLE STRIKE (just recovered)

#### EXALTED MECHANICS:
```
ROUND 1:

Warrior declares: "I attack the brigand with my sword"
GM: "Roll Close Combat"
Warrior: Rolls 3 successes

GM: "You hit. Spend 2 motes for Many-Attacks Technique?"
Warrior: "Yes! Spend 2 motes."
GM: "You make a second attack without flurry penalty. Roll again."
Warrior: Rolls 2 successes

Damage calculated:
- First attack: d10 + 2 (Weapon) = 8 damage (after armor, maybe 5 damage dealt)
- Second attack: d10 + 2 (Weapon) = 6 damage (after armor, maybe 3 damage dealt)
- Total: Brigand takes 8 damage (starts with ~12 HP)

Brigand's turn: Attacks back (normal roll)
```

**Complexity:** Moderate. Multiple rolls, tracking motes, damage calculation.

#### HERO HEAVEN MECHANICS (Simplified):
```
ROUND 1:

Warrior declares: "I attack the brigand"
GM: "Roll Duality Dice"
Warrior: Rolls 10 Hope, 6 Fear (Success)

GM: "You land a solid hit. Do you want to spend 1 Hope for your Charm?"
Warrior: "I hear the blade calling for another strike. Yes, I spend 1 Hope."
GM: "Make a second attack against the same target"
Warrior: Rolls Duality Dice
Result: 8 Hope, 8 Fear (Partial Success)

Damage:
- First attack (Success): d6 + 1 (STR 12) + 1 (Sword) = 4-5 damage
- Second attack (Partial Success): d6 + 1 (STR 12) + 0 (partial) = 2-3 damage
- Total: Brigand takes 6-8 damage (starts with ~15 HP)

Hope tracking (visible): Warrior now has 4 Hope (started at 5)
Brigand's turn: Attacks
```

**Complexity:** Simple. One resource (Hope), clear costs, faster.

#### HERO HEAVEN MECHANICS (Exalted-Lite):
```
ROUND 1:

Warrior declares: "I attack the brigand with my sword"
GM: "Roll Duality Dice"
Warrior: Rolls 10 Hope, 6 Fear (Success)

GM: "Hit. Spend 1 Hope to activate Warrior's Double Strike?"
Warrior: "Yes. I feel the blade's hunger."
GM: "Roll again for second attack, this one at no penalty"
Warrior: Rolls 9 Hope, 7 Fear (Success)

Damage:
- First attack (Success): d8 (base) + 1 (STR bonus) + 1 (Sword bonus) = 5-7 damage
- Second attack (Success, no penalty): d8 + 1 (STR) + 1 (Sword) + 1 (Charm bonus) = 6-8 damage
- Total: Brigand takes 11-15 damage (starts with ~20 HP)

Hope tracking: Warrior now has 4 Hope (started at 5)
Essence pool: Warrior has 1 Essence (unrelated to Charm use, tracks other things?)
Brigand's turn: Attacks
```

**Complexity:** Medium. More detail than Simplified, but clearer damage scaling than full Exalted.

## PART 4: HEALTH & DAMAGE SCALING ACROSS SESSIONS

### WITH EXALTED-LIKE CHARMS:

**Session 2 (Low Power):**
- Hero HP: 15
- Brigand HP: 12
- Hero damage per hit: 4-5 (d6 base + 1 STR)
- Charm Double Strike: +1 second attack (same damage)
- Time to kill brigand: 2-3 hits with both attacks active

**Session 5 (Mid Power):**
- Hero HP: 25
- Multiple brigands HP: 10 each (or 1 captain with 25 HP)
- Hero damage per hit: 6-7 (d8 base + 2 Charm bonus + 1 STR)
- Charm Defense: Reset Defense OR +1 to Defense
- Charm Environmental: Destroy terrain, gain +2 to escape
- Time to kill: Still tactically interesting (3-4 hits per enemy, groups are dangerous)

**Session 8 (High Power):**
- Hero HP: 35
- Special antagonist HP: 30-40
- Hero damage per hit: 8-10 (d8 base + 3 Charm bonus + 1 STR + other factors)
- Charm Apocalyptic: Can destroy whole area (non-combat power)
- Charm Extra Attacks: Make 3-4 attacks per round
- Charm Protection: Reduce environmental damage by half
- Time to kill: 3-5 rounds with mix of attacks (not 1-shot, but clearly overpowering normal enemies)

**Key:** Damage scaling comes from:
1. Larger damage dice (d6 → d8)
2. Bigger Charm bonuses (+1 → +3)
3. More Charm effects per turn (extra attacks, combos)
4. NOT from massive health pools exploding

Health stays reasonable because heroes have OPTIONS (defense, extra actions, terrain control, utility).

## PART 5: DECISION MATRIX

### OPTION A: ADOPT EXALTED WHOLESALE

**Pros:**
- ✅ Proven system (thousands of sessions played)
- ✅ Extensive Charm library already written
- ✅ Clear scaling from Charm Tier 1 → 3
- ✅ Mote mechanics create interesting resource decisions

**Cons:**
- ❌ COMPLEX: Motes, Anima, Power, Essence—too many resources
- ❌ Not designed for "Duality Dice" Hope/Fear integration
- ❌ Prerequisite system (Close Combat 5, etc.) doesn't match Tarim-Shaiel's vibe
- ❌ 3-4 hour learning curve for players
- ❌ Overkill for what we need

**Verdict:** **TOO HEAVY.** Would bog down Hero Heaven.

---

### OPTION B: HERO HEAVEN SIMPLIFIED (Hope Only)

**Charms structured as:**
```
CHARM NAME
Prerequisite: Archetype + Session milestone (recovered session 2-3? 5-6? 8+?)

Cost: X Hope
Effect: [Clear mechanical effect]
Whisper: [What the tool says to the wielder]

R/H/K Discovery Note:
- Session 2 feeling: [What hero thinks is happening]
- Session 5 realization: [What hero starts to question]
- Session 8 crisis: [Full moral weight]
```

**Pros:**
- ✅ SIMPLE: One resource (Hope)
- ✅ Direct Hope ↔ R/H/K connection (spend Hope = follow guidance = lose certainty)
- ✅ Fast to learn (5-minute explanation)
- ✅ Clear scaling (Charm bonuses grow, nothing else complicates)

**Cons:**
- ❌ Less nuance (no Commit vs. Spend distinction)
- ❌ Resource decision-making is simpler
- ❌ Might feel "samey" across all Charms

**Verdict:** **WORKABLE**, but loses some Exalted depth.

---

### OPTION C: HERO HEAVEN EXALTED-LITE (Hope + Essence)

**Charms structured as:**
```
CHARM NAME
Prerequisite: Archetype + Essence requirement (Essence 1-3 for different tiers)

Cost: [Spend 1-2 Hope] OR [Commit 1 Hope until scene end]
Effect: [Mechanical effect]
Essence scaling: [What changes as Essence increases]

Whisper: [Tool's guidance]
R/H/K Discovery: [Moral arc across sessions]
```

**Pros:**
- ✅ Depth of Exalted (Spend vs. Commit creates interesting choices)
- ✅ Clear Essence progression (Session 2 = Essence 1, Session 8 = Essence 3)
- ✅ Scaling is transparent (higher Essence = stronger Charm effects)
- ✅ Still relatively simple (only 2 resources: Hope + Essence)
- ✅ Essence can track "how divine am I becoming" thematically

**Cons:**
- ❌ Slightly more complex than Option B
- ❌ Need to define Essence recovery mechanics
- ❌ Might create "mote math" problems if not careful

**Verdict:** **BEST BALANCE.** Keeps Exalted's depth without full complexity.

## PART 6: SAMPLE CHARM (OPTION C FORMAT)

### Example: WARRIOR'S DOUBLE STRIKE

```
WARRIOR'S DOUBLE STRIKE
Archetype: Warrior (available to others with GM approval)
Essence Required: 1+
First Recovered: Session 2-3 (upon understanding warrior identity)

COST
Spend 1 Hope (immediately consumed)

EFFECT
After a successful attack roll, the hero may make a second attack 
against the same target or a different target in close range. 
This second attack is treated as part of the same action (no flurry penalty).

ESSENCE SCALING
- Essence 1: Second attack has no penalty
- Essence 2: Second attack gains +1 to-hit, AND hero may make a third attack 
  against a different target (third attack has flurry penalty as normal)
- Essence 3: Second and third attacks have no penalty; may make up to Warrior's 
  Essence number of attacks in flurry without standard penalties

CHARM TELL
- Everyone sees: Blade glows RED when Warrior activates this
- Only Warrior hears: "STRIKE AGAIN. PRESS THE ADVANTAGE."

WHISPER EFFECT
Following the guidance (spending Hope): +1 to-hit on second attack
Resisting the guidance (not spending Hope): Hero can still attack, but no bonus

R/H/K DISCOVERY ARC

Session 2-3 (Aspirational R/H/K):
  "I'm a Warrior. Warriors fight with strength and speed."
  Warrior thinks: "This Charm IS me. I'm meant to attack multiple times."
  Feels: Perfectly aligned. Hero follows eagerly.

Session 4-5 (Realization):
  Blade whispers for second strike during parley scene (wrong moment).
  Warrior resists: "No. This isn't the time."
  Hope return signals: "You're learning when NOT to attack matters."
  Warrior questions: "Wait... am I a Warrior, or am I just an instrument of violence?"

Session 6-7 (Discovery):
  Blade hungers for finishing blow against wounded enemy.
  Warrior refuses: "That would be murder, not combat."
  Warrior realizes: "I'm not defined by how many times I strike.
                     I'm defined by choosing WHEN to strike.
                     That's actually warrior strength."

Session 8+ (Actual R/H/K Locked):
  Actual Warrior identity: "I'm someone who fights with PURPOSE.
                            Not just speed, but WISDOM about when power is justified."
  Actual Resist: Attacks that are unnecessary or dishonorable
  Actual Hunger: For worthy opponents and meaningful combat
  Actual Know: The difference between warrior and murderer
```

## PART 7: RECOMMENDATION

**For Hero Heaven, I recommend: OPTION C (Exalted-Lite with Hope + Essence)**

**Reasoning:**

1. **Complexity Sweet Spot:** More depth than bare Hope system, but not overwhelming
2. **Thematic Alignment:** Essence tracks "how divine am I becoming" which mirrors the narrative arc
3. **Scaling Transparency:** Essence 1 → 3 progression is clear and feels like power growth
4. **Hope ↔ R/H/K Connection Preserved:** Spending Hope on Charms still feels like moral choice
5. **Adapted from Proven System:** Exalted's structure works; we're just simplifying
6. **Playable in 10-15 minutes:** Character knows "Spend Hope for Charm, Essence gates higher tiers"

**Implementation Path:**

1. Define Essence recovery: When do heroes gain Essence 2? (Session 4-5 milestone?)
2. Create 6 Charm trees (one per archetype)
3. Design 3 Charms per archetype per Essence tier (18 total Charms to start)
4. Test with Session 2 combat → Session 8 combat examples
5. Lock down R/H/K discovery arcs for each Charm
