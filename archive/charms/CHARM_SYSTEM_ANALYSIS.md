## HISTORICAL DESIGN (January 2026 - Deprecated)

The following represents the **hybrid resource system** that was explored and ultimately abandoned in favor of Hope-only costs for simpler integration with Daggerheart's native mechanics.

### Hybrid Resource System (DEPRECATED)

Tarim-Shaiel's Charm system initially explored three key resources:

1. **Fatigue Points (FP) - Tactical Resource**
   - Derived from Health stat
   - Primary method of Charm activation
   - Enabled frequent, low-stakes Charm use
   - Recovered between scenes (with "short" or "long" rest)

2. **Will Points - Strategic Resource**
   - Derived from Will stat
   - Used for powerful Tier 2+ Charms
   - Represented character's conviction
   - Recovered at significant narrative moments (reflecting R/H/K Decisions)

3. **Hope - Narrative/Mythic Resource**
   - Native to Daggerheart—integrated here for consistency
   - Gained through narrative interactions
   - Used for high-impact Tier 3 Charms
   - Signaled character's alignment and growth
   - Sparse, strategic spending

### Old Cost Structure (DEPRECATED)

#### Tier 1 Charms
- **Cost:** 2 FP OR 1 Hope
- **Examples:**
  - Warrior's Perfect Strike
  - Seeker's Truth Sense
  - Bridge's Empathy

#### Tier 2 Charms
- **Cost:** 3 FP OR 1 Hope + 1 Will
- **Examples:**
  - Warrior's Iron Defense
  - Breaker's Systematic Unraveling
  - Seeker's Power Tracing

#### Tier 3 Charms
- **Cost:** 2-3 Hope (Hope-only)
- **Examples:**
  - Warrior's Celestial Fury
  - Breaker's Apocalyptic Potential
  - Sacrificer's Resurrection

### Why This System Was Abandoned

**Problems Identified:**
1. **Too Complex:** Three resources to track (Hope, FP, Will) when Daggerheart only uses Hope
2. **GURPS Dependency:** Required importing GURPS stats (Health, Will) which weren't needed for Daggerheart integration
3. **Conversion Issues:** "1 Hope = 3 FP" conversion created cognitive load
4. **Inconsistent with Daggerheart:** Daggerheart's spell costs use Hope directly, hybrid system felt alien

**Solution:**
- Simplified to **Hope-only** costs (1-3 Hope per Charm)
- Dropped FP and Will entirely
- Better integration with Daggerheart's native resource economy
- Clearer progression with 4-tier system matching Daggerheart levels

## DESIGN EVOLUTION

### January 2026: Hybrid System
- Explored FP+Will+Hope triple resource
- 3-tier progression (Tier 1/2/3)
- Session-based unlocking (Sessions 2-3, 5-6, 8+)

### February 2026: Simplified System
- Hope-only costs (1-3 Hope)
- 4-tier progression matching Daggerheart (Levels 1, 2-4, 5-7, 8-10)
- Narrative-driven awakening (not session-locked)
- Recall cost system (0/1/2/3 Hope)
