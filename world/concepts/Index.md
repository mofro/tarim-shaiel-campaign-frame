# Concepts & Forces Index

Master registry of abstract forces, cosmological structures, mechanisms, and named phenomena — things that are not characters or locations but have relational weight in the world. These are the connective tissue between factions, events, and places.

**Category key:**
- `place` — real location with cosmological significance
- `force` — active cosmic/magical power or pressure
- `system` — infrastructure, magical or physical
- `mechanism` — a specific magical operation or process
- `entity` — being or collective without faction structure

**Status key:** `canon` | `draft` | `proposed` | `tbd`
