# Campaign Present: Faction Power Map (~1200 CE Equivalent)

## Overview

This document provides a **detailed snapshot** of the political landscape when the heroes return to the mortal world. It complements `HISTORICAL_TIMELINE.md` by focusing on the **current state** rather than historical progression.

**Temporal Context:** Campaign Present (~1200-1250 CE equivalent)
- 1,000 years after heroes' legendary deeds
- World at economic/cultural peak (before Lich-Legion threat revealed)
- No single hegemon; multiple power centers in complex balance
