## What Is Known (Canon)

The Scholar's Purge occurred at 1173 HB, near the end of the Gathering Storm epoch and approximately 25 years before the heroes' return. It follows "Universities Flourish" (1078) and "The Grey Council" (1118) in the causal chain, and immediately precedes "Mysterious Disappearances Begin" (1138 — *note: chronologically prior; see timeline for ordering*).

The nature of the purge — who enacted it, who was targeted, what survived — is not yet defined.

## Design Notes

- **Model:** Byzantine dispersal (knowledge scattered, not destroyed) — NOT the Chinese burning-and-burying model
- **Narrative implication:** The knowledge survived. It is recoverable. Someone knows where the pieces are.
- **Faction hook:** `scholars-remnant` is a confirmed Priority 1 faction. This event is likely their origin or defining trauma.
- **Wizard connection:** The Wizard's awareness of the purge's aftermath — and whether he orchestrated it, exploited it, or is hunting the scattered knowledge — is an open design question tied to Decision 4 (Wizard motivation).
