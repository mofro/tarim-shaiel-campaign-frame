# Kucha

A sacred city where Buddhism thrives. The nearby Kizil Caves contain some of the Silk Road's greatest artistic treasures—Buddhist sculptures, murals, and paintings spanning centuries.

## Historical Basis

**Tarim Basin** – Major Buddhist state with strong monastic traditions. Home to famous monasteries and the Kizil Caves complex, a pilgrimage destination.

## Key Features

- **Kizil Caves Complex** – Thousands of Buddhist artworks carved into cliff faces
- **Active Monasteries** – Major center of Buddhist learning and spiritual practice
- **Artistic Treasures** – Sculptures, murals, illuminated manuscripts

## Spiritual Significance

A destination for Buddhist pilgrims seeking enlightenment and study. The caves are considered sacred, with each chamber hosting different deities and teaching narratives.

## Factions

- Buddhist Monastic Community (dominant)
- Pilgrimage guides
- Local craftspeople

## Resources

- Buddhist textiles and robes
- Religious artwork and sculpture
- Sacred manuscripts
