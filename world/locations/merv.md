# Merv (Mary)

A city haunted by its own history. Once a major oasis hub, it was destroyed by Mongol forces and rebuilt as a smaller settlement. The ruins still draw those seeking lost knowledge.

## Historical Basis

**Margiana** – Major oasis city destroyed by Jochi Khan (son of Genghis Khan). Eventually rebuilt as the smaller settlement of Mary, never regaining former importance.

## Current State

- **Partially Rebuilt** – Mary village exists but is shadow of former city
- **Ruins Prominent** – Ancient structures still visible in landscape
- **Refuge for Survivors** – Those rebuilding from the ashes

## Factions

- Turkmen locals (primary)
- Rebuilding merchants
- Archaeological expeditions

## Connections

Waypoint on routes between western Central Asia and eastern territories.
