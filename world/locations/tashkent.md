# Tashkent

The largest city in Central Asia. A cosmopolitan center where traders, warriors, and scholars converge.

## Historical Basis

**Fergana Valley** – Turkic-origin city that became Islamic around the 12th-13th centuries. Survived Mongol conquest and remained a major regional power.

## Key Features

- **Stone City Fortifications** – Massive defensive walls and gates
- **Agricultural Center** – Fertile Fergana Valley provides abundant resources
- **Trade Hub** – Largest commercial center in Central Asia

## Factions

- Central Asian Trade Union (dominant)
- Regional merchant families
- Military garrison

## Resources

- Agricultural goods (grain, horses)
- Crafted goods
- Trade services
