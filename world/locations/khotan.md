# Khotan

A mountain kingdom whose wealth flows from green jade. Buddhist traditions persist even as Islamic influence grows. Merchants seek Khotan jade above all other riches.

## Historical Basis

**Tarim Basin** – Originally a Buddhist state with strong monastic traditions. Gradually converted to Islam, but cultural and religious syncretism remains. Renowned for jade working.

## Key Features

- **Jade Capital** – World-renowned source of highest-quality jade
- **Oasis Settlement** – Fertile valley surrounded by mountains and desert
- **Cultural Syncretism** – Buddhist and Islamic traditions coexist

## Factions

- Jade Merchants Guild (economic power)
- Buddhist Monastic Order (cultural/religious influence)
- Local government

## Resources

- Jade (finest quality on Silk Road)
- Hand-made carpets
- Oasis agricultural goods

## Trade

Jade exports command premium prices everywhere. A single jade carving from Khotan can fund a merchant's entire venture.
