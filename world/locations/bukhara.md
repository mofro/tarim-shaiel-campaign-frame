# Bukhara

A city of scholars and merchants. Once devastated by Mongol conquest, it rose again as a center of Islamic learning and commerce.

## Historical Basis

**Sogdiana** – Originally Zoroastrian, later converted to Islam. Survived Mongol invasion and was revived as a major intellectual center with extraordinary architecture.

## Key Features

- **140+ Architectural Monuments** – Testament to wealth and cultural importance
- **Scholarly Hub** – Major center of Islamic learning and religious study
- **Trade Center** – Critical nexus on the Silk Road

## Factions

- Islamic Scholarly Network (primary influence)
- Local merchant guilds
- Regional authorities

## Resources

- High-quality textiles
- Scholarly manuscripts
- Luxury trade goods
