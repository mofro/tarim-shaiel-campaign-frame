**KEY REALIZATIONS FROM THIS SESSION:**

1. **Secret god-tier snippets during Session 0 awakenings solve the core investment problem.** Players get personal mystery (why does THIS feel familiar?) without collapsing the campaign mystery (why were we all expelled?).

2. **The dual-truth system requires absolute clarity about what's hidden vs. revealed.** Snippets work because they're emotional truth, not explanatory truth. GM knows what happened; players only feel the weight of loss.

3. **Event-centric flashback design avoids combinatorial explosion.** Instead of pre-scripting 15 pair-specific shared memories (6 factorial), script 8-12 critical events that work with any pairing. Triggers are organic, not mechanical.

4. **Archetype descriptions must describe the person NOW, not hint at their past.** They're about psychological patterns and role function, not about revealing fallen godhood. Discovery happens in play, not in character creation.

5. **The pitch must match the opacity level.** Don't hint at the mystery you're trying to preserve. State facts (heroes emerged 200 years ago, they earned paradise, the world lives with the consequences). Let players draw their own conclusions.

6. **Session 0 emotional arc is complete and satisfying:**
   - Individual awakenings with personal god-tier snippets (player-to-character investment)
   - Convergence on caravan (party formation)
   - Campfire recognition moment (shared mystery revelation)
   - Cut to black on realization, not explanation (emotional cliffhanger)
   - Total time: 4.5-5 hours

7. **The runsheet is executable:** Hybrid pacing with Round 1 & 2 parallel (Bridge+Seeker, Warrior+Sacrificer), Round 3 sequential (Breaker), Round 4 pulls to convergence (Visionary crisis), settling and recognition.
