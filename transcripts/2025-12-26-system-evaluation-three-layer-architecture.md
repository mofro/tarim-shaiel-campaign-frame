## LORE KEEPER CONFIRMING THE ARCHITECTURE

### **The Real Three-Layer Integration**

**Layer 1: Conflict Resolution (Daggerheart Duality Dice)**
- Roll, determine outcome, move on
- No mechanical ambiguity

**Layer 2: Power Progression (Exalted Charms, Archetype-Aligned)**
- Each Charm has an alignment profile: Archetype-aligned, misaligned (hunger), or independent
- **ALIGNED guidance** → Follow it = mechanical bonus
- **MISALIGNED guidance** → Resist it = Hope return + confirmation of alignment
- **INDEPENDENT guidance** → No mechanical bonus/penalty, but narrative consequences

**Layer 3: Narrative Mechanics (R/H/K + Secret Tool Whispers)**
- Only the wielder hears the tool's whisper; no obligation to explain
- Hope is visible; reasons are secret
- Archetype abilities can constrain other heroes (cost: Hope)
- Trust between heroes is narrative/roleplay level

## WHAT THIS ACTUALLY MEANS

You've described a system where:

✅ **Mechanical transparency:** Everyone knows how Charms work, what they cost, what they do
✅ **Narrative ambiguity:** Nobody knows what the tool whispered or why the wielder chose to follow it
✅ **Player agency:** Every choice is the wielder's, with clear costs; refusal is always an option
✅ **Complicity & shame:** If I follow the blade's secret guidance and good things happen, I might never explain. The party trusts me *despite incomplete information*.

## WHY THIS MATTERS

✅ **Mirrors the Narrative:** Heroes expelled from paradise with incomplete understanding. They discover themselves through moral choice.

✅ **Makes Self-Discovery Mechanical:** Tools act as mirrors. By choosing to follow/resist whispers, heroes discover who they actually are.

✅ **Every Choice is Identity-Defining:** Will you follow the hunger? In choosing, what do you learn about yourself?

- **Mechanical clarity:** Heroes know what following/resisting costs (Hope, bonuses, etc.)
- **Narrative ambiguity:** Heroes don't know if they're choosing right until consequences reveal it
- **Self-discovery:** Heroes don't know who they are; they discover through play
