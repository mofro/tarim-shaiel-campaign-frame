## Environment Stat Block

> *Use when the heroes are operating within territory where this force is actively manifesting — a region, an event, a scene where the condition is the encounter. This is not a creature stat block. The heroes are inside the condition; the condition does not have turns.*

```daggerheart
name: [Force/Condition Name]
difficulty: 16                  # Range 14-20 for divine-scale conditions; raise as the campaign progresses
tone: [Adjective], [Adjective]  # e.g. "Wrong-directed, Suffocating" — the felt register of being inside this condition
impulses:
  - "On a 1-5: [Fear consequence — what the force does when heroes are afraid or have failed]"
  - "On a 6-10: [Pressure builds — intermediate consequence; the force gains ground without dramatic action]"
  - "On an 11+: [Brief reprieve or clarity — what briefly abates when heroes act decisively]"
adversaries:
  - "[Primary manifestation — what creature, corrupted NPC, or phenomenon appears within this condition]"
  - "[Secondary manifestation — what accompanies or follows the primary]"
features:
  - name: [Feature Name]
    type: Passive
    desc: |
      [Ongoing effect of this condition on hero rolls, the environment, or NPCs present. A permanent ambient pressure while heroes are inside it.]
      GM question: [Specific prompt — what does this look like in this region or season? How is it different from baseline?]
  - name: [Feature Name]
    type: Active
    desc: |
      [What the condition does at specific narrative or mechanical triggers — when a hero uses a particular ability, when a threshold is crossed, when the situation worsens.]
      GM question: [How does this manifest differently here versus another location where this force is present?]
  - name: [Feature Name]
    type: Escalation
    desc: |
      [What happens when the force gains significant ground — repeated hero failures, worsening cosmological situation, or proximity to a key story beat. This should feel qualitatively different from the Active feature, not just larger.]
      GM question: [What is the visible sign that this force is winning in this specific place? What do the locals call it?]
```
