### Detailed Charm Description
Diplomatic Resonance allows a Bridge character to transcend typical communication barriers. More than a simple persuasion technique, this charm represents a deep empathetic connection that can momentarily align seemingly irreconcilable perspectives. It draws from the Bridge's ability to see multiple sides of a conflict, finding the thread of shared humanity that connects ostensibly different groups.
