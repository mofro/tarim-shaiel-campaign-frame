## Overview

This document contains complete information about **Hero Heaven (The Celestial Peak)**, its threshold between mortal world and celestial realm, and the mechanics of accessing/defending it. This is the ultimate objective of the Wizard's 1,000-year plan and the site of the campaign's potential climax.

**Framing note:** The threshold is not merely a door to a reward. It is a critical node in the mythic ecosystem — a pressure regulator between mortal and celestial energy. The Wizard's plan to breach it by force is not just a heist; it is an assault on a load-bearing structure of reality. This framing must be central to how this document is developed. See [[STORY_ARC_SYNTHESIS]] §“The Mythic Ecosystem” and §“How the Wizard Threatens It” before developing any section below.

### 5. Campaign Endgame Scenarios

**Scenario 1: Defend the Threshold**
- Wizard launches Lich-Legion assault on threshold location
- Heroes must hold position while allies arrive
- Multi-wave battle: undead armies, Liches, finally Wizard
- Outcome: Threshold protected, Wizard defeated, heroes' charge complete

**Scenario 2: Seal the Threshold**
- Wizard already breaching threshold; too late to stop
- Heroes must choose: Let him through or seal permanently
- Sealing requires sacrifice (one hero, all heroes, something else?)
- Outcome: Threshold destroyed, no one can access Hero Heaven again

**Scenario 3: Negotiate/Redeem**
- Confront Wizard before final assault
- Discover his original motivation (was it justified?)
- Attempt to redeem him or offer alternative
- Very difficult; requires exceptional player choices
- Outcome: Wizard stands down, becomes tragic ally or exiled

**Scenario 4: Partial Victory**
- Defeat Wizard but threshold damaged
- Hero Heaven accessible but corrupted/unstable
- Must choose: Repair (how?) or abandon
- Long-term consequences for cosmology

**Scenario 5: Player-Driven Alternative**
- Heroes devise unexpected solution
- Use threshold for different purpose
- Redefine what "heroism" means in this world
- Outcome: Transcend binary win/lose

### 6. Consequences of Different Outcomes

**If Wizard Succeeds:**
- Gains celestial power (becomes god-like threat)
- Hero Heaven corrupted or destroyed
- Cosmic balance disrupted (afterlife system collapses?)
- World faces existential threat beyond Lich-Legion

**If Threshold Sealed:**
- No more earned rewards for heroism
- Heroes cannot return to Hero Heaven
- Future heroes have no celestial reward
- World must find new meaning for heroism

**If Wizard Defeated:**
- Threshold restored to original function
- Heroes complete charge, re-earn entry
- Can they choose to stay in mortal world instead?
- What happens to Lich-Legion without Wizard?

**If Wizard Redeemed:**
- Unprecedented outcome (cosmic precedent)
- His knowledge could benefit world
- But can he be trusted? Supervised?
- Lich Cadre must be dealt with separately
