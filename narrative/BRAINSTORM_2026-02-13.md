## SILK ROAD MYTHOLOGY & KEY LOCATIONS

**Question:** We stopped short of making a list of places important to reality, mythology, and storytelling. Era-appropriate key locations from tales, religion, and history to feed our religions, myths, and locations.

### Real-World Silk Road Mythological Sites

**Religious/Spiritual:**
- **Jerusalem:** Abrahamic holy city
- **Mecca/Medina:** Islamic pilgrimage sites
- **Varanasi:** Hindu sacred city on Ganges
- **Bodh Gaya:** Where Buddha achieved enlightenment
- **Mount Kailash:** Sacred to Hindus, Buddhists, Jains, Bon
- **Lhasa:** Tibetan Buddhist holy city
- **Chang'an (Xi'an):** Eastern terminus, multiple religions converge

**Legendary/Mythological:**
- **Shambhala:** Hidden Buddhist paradise
- **Kunlun Mountains:** Chinese mythological paradise, Queen Mother of the West
- **Taklamakan Desert:** "Desert of Death," ghost stories, lost cities
- **Gobi Desert:** Dinosaur fossils (ancient "dragon bones"), sandstorms
- **Pamir Mountains:** "Roof of the World," extreme geography
- **Lake Baikal:** Sacred to shamanic traditions, unique ecosystem

**Historical Trade Cities (With Mythological Aspects):**
- **Samarkand:** Legendary beauty, "Rome of the East"
- **Bukhara:** "Noble Bukhara," center of learning
- **Kashgar:** Western gateway, oasis city, cultural crossroads
- **Dunhuang:** Cave temples, manuscript discoveries
- **Turfan:** Below sea level, extreme heat, unique culture
- **Balkh:** "Mother of Cities," ancient beyond memory

**Natural Wonders:**
- **Tian Shan Mountains:** "Celestial Mountains"
- **Aral Sea:** (Historical - now ecological disaster, but was major water body)
- **Caspian Sea:** Landlocked sea, unique ecosystem
- **Ganges River:** Sacred river, life-giver
- **Yellow River/Yangtze:** Chinese civilization cradles
- **Indus River:** Another civilization cradle
