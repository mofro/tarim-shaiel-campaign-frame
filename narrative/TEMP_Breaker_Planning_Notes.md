## Breaker Core Concept

### Archetype Comparison
**Warrior doubt:** "Can I protect without dominating?"
**Breaker doubt:** "Can I be present without destroying everything I touch?"

### Structural Parallels with Warrior
- Both wake in vulnerable situations
- Both have tools that *want* to be used
- Both face moment where using tool seems obvious
- Both must choose restraint despite capability

### Key Difference
- **Warrior:** "Don't use the weapon" (avoid threatening others - social threat)
- **Breaker:** "Don't break the wrong thing" (avoid destroying escape route - structural threat)

### Initial Framework

**Setting Ideas:**
- Trapped location (collapsed building? cave-in? locked structure?)
- Others trapped with Breaker
- Obvious escape route has fatal flaw

**Tool Options:**
- Hammer (classic breaking tool)
- Chisel (precision breaking)
- Bare hands that are too strong (internal tool)
- Something crude/improvised

**Core Crisis:**
- Breaking free the obvious way destroys what keeps others alive
- Must find precise/careful way instead of powerful way
- Restraint = choosing precision over power

**Alignment Memory:**
- Moment of choosing NOT to break when breaking feels natural
- Or: Choosing surgical precision over overwhelming force
- Echo: "I've held back this power before"
