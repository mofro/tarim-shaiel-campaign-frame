# CELESTIAL DICE MECHANICS

**Last Updated:** 2026-02-09 (updated to 4-tier Daggerheart system)
**Maintained by:** Lore Keeper & Claude

## OVERVIEW

Celestial dice mechanics form the core of Tarim-Shaiel's narrative and mechanical progression, integrating Daggerheart's duality system with our unique mythic awakening framework.

## DUALITY DICE FOUNDATION

### Basic Mechanism
- Rolls use d12 Hope/Fear dice
- Hope represents potential and divine possibility
- Fear represents mortal limitations and doubt

### Resolution Principles
- Rolling with Hope: Advantage on success
- More Hope dice = Greater narrative potential
- Mythic characters accumulate Hope through choices

## TIER PROGRESSION (Daggerheart Integration)

**Daggerheart Structure:** 4 tiers (Tier 1 = Level 1, Tier 2 = Levels 2-4, Tier 3 = Levels 5-7, Tier 4 = Levels 8-10)

### Tier 0: Dormant (Pre-Awakening)
- Standard duality dice (1d12 Hope + 1d12 Fear)
- No Charm access
- Standard Daggerheart mechanics

### Tier 1 (Level 1): First Awakening
- Celestial dice: 2d12 Hope (keep highest) + 1d12 Fear when using tool
- Access to Tier 1 Charms
- Hope bonuses: +1 Hope per scene
- Recall cost: 0 Hope

### Tier 2 (Levels 2-4): Expanding Consciousness
- Celestial dice: 3d12 Hope (keep highest) + 1d12 Fear when aligned with purpose
- Access to Tier 2 Charms + enhanced Tier 1
- Hope bonuses: +2 Hope per scene
- Recall cost: 1 Hope

### Tier 3 (Levels 5-7): Purpose Clarity
- Celestial dice: 3d12 Hope (keep highest) + 1d12 Fear always active
- Access to Tier 3 Charms + maximum Tier 2 scaling
- Hope bonuses: +2 Hope per scene
- Recall cost: 2 Hope

### Tier 4 (Levels 8-10): Full Awakening
- Celestial dice: 3d12 Hope (keep highest) + 1d12 Fear + mythic bonuses
- Access to Tier 4 Charms + maximum scaling all tiers
- Hope bonuses: +3 Hope per scene
- Recall cost: 3 Hope

## HOPE ECONOMY

### Hope Gain Mechanisms

**1. Duality Dice Rolls**
- Roll with Hope (Hope > Fear): +1 Hope
- Frequency: Approximately 50% of rolls

**2. Whisper Resistance**
- Resist tool guidance: +1 Hope (always)
- Signals character's growing self-understanding
- Frequency: 1-3 times per session

**3. Mythic Awakening Bonuses**
- **Tier 1 (Level 1):** +1 Hope per scene
- **Tier 2 (Levels 2-4):** +2 Hope per scene
- **Tier 3 (Levels 5-7):** +2 Hope per scene
- **Tier 4 (Levels 8-10):** +3 Hope per scene
- Represents increasing divine potential

**4. Ally Interactions**
- Other players can gift Hope
- Represents collaborative mythic growth

### Hope Spending

**Tier 1 Spending**
- Minor narrative adjustments
- Simple Charm activations
- Tactical rerolls

**Tier 2 Spending**
- Moderate narrative interventions
- Complex Charm activations
- Combining multiple action types

**Tier 3 Spending**
- Massive narrative transformations
- Apocalyptic Charm activations
- Rewriting immediate reality

## TOOL WHISPER INTERACTIONS

### Whisper Types
1. **Guidance Whispers:** Suggest action
2. **Revelation Whispers:** Provide hidden information
3. **Transformation Whispers:** Offer profound change

### Interaction Mechanics
- Following whisper: Advantage dice
- Resisting whisper: Hope gain
- Partial following: Nuanced narrative consequences

## FINAL NOTES

The celestial dice system is not just a mechanical resolution tool, but a narrative discovery mechanism. Each roll represents a moment of mythic potential, where mortal limitations can be transcended through conscious choice.
